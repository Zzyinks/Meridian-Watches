The process

How your
watch gets
made.

01
You choose every detail
Dial colour, case size, case shape, bracelet or strap, material finish. You tell me exactly what you want — I'll guide you through options and combinations that work best.
02
Parts are sourced for your build
Every component is ordered specifically for your watch. No inventory sitting around. The NH35 movement at the heart of your piece is genuine Japanese precision — proven across millions of watches.
03
Assembled by hand
I build your watch myself, by hand, at my bench in Montréal. Every part is fitted, tested, and checked before it ships. You know who made it — because only one person did.
04
Delivered to your door
Your watch ships ready to wear. $240–$350 depending on specs. Turnaround is typically 2–3 weeks from order confirmation.
